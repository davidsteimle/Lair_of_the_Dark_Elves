BACKGROUND STORY

THE FELL BLADE

Grief's Infernal Flower is a cursed short sword +1, which the weilder must use above all other weapons. In fact, when trying to weild any other weapon, the user finds themselves unable to grasp the new weapon, and tools or improvised weapons prove useless once their intent is clear to the blade (Will contest).
